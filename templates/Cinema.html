About Us

AITUPARK

Currently, a movie theater in the cinema industry is not a mere public facility with an auditorium. Apart from a nice
display picture, the key requirements of today's moviegoers also include an opportunity to choose a movie at any time
convenient for them, a seat where they could stretch their legs to spend comfortably 1.5 hours watching a movie in
a cosy and stylish place, and where they could have some delicious refreshment prior to the screening. Do many
motion-picture theaters meet such high expectations? Absolutely not. Hence, the AITUPARK Theatres network stands
distinctive, being the leading cinema in Kazakhstan, and rightfully so. The AITUPARK Theatres cinema theaters
already meet the highest requirements laid for the new generation cinemas: a modern stylish design, high
technologies to ensure the best sound and picture, cosy and ergonomic chairs, including seats for persons with
special needs. However, as you know, there is no limit to perfection. AITUPARK Theatres has been making an
important contribution to Kazakhstan's cultural heritage by strengthening the Country 's standing in the global cinema
industry. AITUPARK Theatres is viewed as a reliable and responsible business partner for a reason.

Competitive advantage: an inimitable style and investments in the quality

AITUPARK Theatres with its network of AITUPARK cinema theaters is the largest cinema theatrical company in
Central Asia, which offers a unique business model for the entertainment industry and related services. To create the
new quality product, AITUPARK Theatres believes using solely the state-of- the-art silver screen technologies to be
its top priority. So, the Company was the first one in Kazakhstan that introduced the Dolby Atmos audacious audio
system, IMAX format and laser projectors in its halls.

The AITUPARK Theatres network was established in 2005 by a team of high performers. Considering the experience
and level offered to the audience by the world's best cinemas, it was decided to create the ideal conditions for
Kazakhstani filmgoers too, for each cinema theater to become the archetype of quality in the entertainment and
leisure industry.

The network's cinema theaters are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and provide a wide range of services:

  • Concessions
  • Lounge Bars
  • Original Version
  • Dine-in Cinemas.

The Company has been investing much in the construction and technological infrastructure of the new cinema theaters,
as well as advance training of its personnel.

The successful business of AITUPARK Theatres is ensured by:

  • The construction and finishing of the cinema halls
  • Fitting the complex with mechanical, electrical and sanitary engineering systems
  • Installing the latest audio and film projection equipment
  • Training and retraining of its personnel through engagement in workshops and
    practical studies organized by the industry's leaders
  • Modern facilities for the bars and cashier desks.

Within less than 3 years, AITUPARK Theatres has become the leader in the Country's market, as far as the number of
screens in the largest cities is concerned. Our first cinema theaters. Now the network covers all the largest cities and
is a 100% Kazakhstani company. At present, the AITUPARK Theatres network has 72 halls and 10,147 seats.
